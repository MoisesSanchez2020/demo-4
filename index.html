About Us

Our Company

NouvaTech, Inc. is a minority-owned business based in San Juan & Humacao, Puerto Rico, specializing in Software Development, IT Consulting, and Cloud Solutions. We bring together deep technical expertise and business insight to deliver transformative technology solutions for clients across industries.

Our core services include Microsoft Dynamics 365 implementations, Azure and AWS infrastructure management, mobile platform development, and software testing. We also offer hosted Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) solutions and system integrations powered by secure cloud architectures.

At NouvaTech, we partner with leading technology providers such as Microsoft, MRC 360, and Cloud Titan to deliver enterprise-grade results for both government and private organizations. Our proven track record in digital transformation, automation, and security ensures clients stay agile, compliant, and ready for tomorrow.
